The engineering team at DreamFactory designed and built some of the very first applications that use web services.Over the years we made many mistakes trying to create the perfect mobile backend for these applications.In our experience talking to customers, we’ve learned that many companies face the same challenges we’ve had to think about and tackle over the years.One of the biggest challenges is figuring out a winning API strategy.This blog post lays out some of the traps and pitfalls that companies often experience when they decide to build their own REST APIs.
