Applications – and specifically mobile apps – occupy a key battleground for companies trying to woo customers, differentiate their products and drive growth.This is happening across many industries but banking provides a good example.Mobile applications that put banking services in the palm of your hand have become a much more important differentiator than interest rates, which were previously used to lure customers.A well-designed mobile app drives a more engaging experience for customers and this, in turn, drives customer acquisition and retention.During the recent Apps World show in San Francisco, we saw some examples of this trend and the extraordinary growth right across the application ecosystem.
