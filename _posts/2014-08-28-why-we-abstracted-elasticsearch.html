“Databases.As a Service.” adorns the Orchestrate home page.We’ve emphasized the plural because we run our service on multiple persistent stores, and a key piece of what makes up Orchestrate is how we expose this software, for example, Elasticsearch.A recent Elasticsearch vulnerability saw Amazon EC2 nodes hijacked for denial-of-service attacks because of improperly secured instances, which allowed hackers to inject malicious scripts on the affected machine.
