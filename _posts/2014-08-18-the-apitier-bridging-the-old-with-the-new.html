I am still recovering from a packed four-day agenda at the Gartner Catalyst conference in San Diego.I had numerous interesting and often revealing conversations with enterprise IT leaders who are transforming their large and complex enterprises into agile and digital businesses.And yes, not to forget all the late night parties and the after-parties in the scenic southern California coast.However, the most interesting aspect of Catalyst was the convergence of security architects and application development professionals at one location and to see how app-dev and security discussions are converging.Not surprisingly, the main theme of discussion was the growing digital economy and how old enterprises need to quickly respond to the growing demands of the various digital channels, among them, mobile, cloud and the Internet of Things (IoT).
