Getting listed on the AWS Marketplace is an important milestone in the history of SlashDB because having your product featured on this particular marketplace is difficult due to standards and criteria that have to be met.What does this mean for you (and your databases)?SlashDB drastically shortens the time to market for on-demand data products and web APIs and makes secure, authenticated access to databases via XML, JSON, CSV and HTML quick and easy.AWS Marketplace is an online store that makes it easy for you to find and immediately start using the software and services that run on the AWS Cloud.You can use AWS Marketplace’s 1-Click deployment to quickly launch SlashDB.
