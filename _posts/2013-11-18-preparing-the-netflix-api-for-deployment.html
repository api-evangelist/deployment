At Netflix, we are committed to bringing new features and product enhancements to customers rapidly and frequently.Consequently, dozens of engineering teams are constantly innovating on their services, resulting in a rate of change to the overall service is vast and unending.Because of our appetite for providing such improvements to our members, it is critical for us to maintain a Software Delivery pipeline that allows us to deploy changes to our production environments in an easy, seamless, and quick way, with minimal risk.The Netflix API is responsible for distributing content that supports our features to the UIs.The API in turn, is dependent on a large number of services that are responsible for generating the data.
